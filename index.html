For your health

A whole foods plant-based diet has been shown to prevent the leading causes of deaths in the United States: heart disease, diabetes, and cancer. Check out the following documentaries on Netflix:

  • What The Health
  • The Game Changers

It takes 22 days to break a habit, take the 22 day vegan challenge today! And find local vegan options near you at HappyCow.net.

For our environment

Animals raised for agriculture and animals caught from the ocean contribute to the climate crisis. Check out the following documentaries on Netflix:

  • Cowspiracy
  • Seaspiracy

For the animals

They suffer immensely for food, clothing, cosmetics, science, and entertainment. When we walk a mile in their shoes, what would they want? See for yourself:

  • Earthlings
  • Dominion

Become an advocate

There are a ton of ways to speak up for animals such as through art, food, music, dance, disruptions, and outreach. Consider volunteering or donating to animal rights organizations such as:

  • PETA
  • Anonymous for the Voiceless
  • Animal Save Movement
  • Direct Action Everywhere
  • Vegan Outreach
  • Agriculture Fairness Alliance
  • Rancher Advocacy Program
If you want guidance on how to become an advocate, check out the Animal Activism Mentorship program.
