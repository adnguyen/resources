For your health

A balanced plant-based diet lets you survive and thrive, wile preventing the most common diseases like heart disease, diabetes, and cancer. See for yourself on Netflix:

  • What The Health
  • The Game Changers

It takes ~20 days to break a habit, so get the 21 day starter kit from the Physician's Committee for Responsible Medicine (PCRM) or take the 22 day challenge today! And find local plant-based options near you at HappyCow.net.

For our environment

Animals raised for agriculture and animals caught from the ocean contribute to the environmental crisis. See for yourself on Netflix:

  • Cowspiracy
  • Seaspiracy

For the animals

They suffer immensely for food, clothing, cosmetics, science, and entertainment. If you were to walk a mile in their shoes, then what would you want? See for yourself on YouTube:

  • Earthlings
  • Dominion
Or visit your local animal sanctuary so you can connect with them directly!

Become an advocate

There are a ton of ways to speak up for animals through art, food, music, dance, disruptions, and outreach. Consider donating to animal rights organizations:

  • PETA
  • Mercy for Animals
  • Rise for Animals
  • Animal Outlook
  • Vegan Outreach
  • Agriculture Fairness Alliance
  • Rancher Advocacy Program
Consider volunteering your time for grassroots advocacy:
  • Anonymous for the Voiceless
  • Animal Save Movement
  • Direct Action Everywhere
If you want guidance on how to become an advocate, check out the Animal Activism Mentorship program.
