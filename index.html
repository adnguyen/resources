For your health

A balanced plant-based diet enables you to survive and thrive, while preventing the most common diseases like heart disease, diabetes, and cancer. See for yourself on Netflix:

  • What The Health
  • The Game Changers

It takes ~20 days to break a habit, so get the 21 day starter kit from the Physician's Committee for Responsible Medicine (PCRM) or take the 22 day challenge today! Eating out? Find local plant-based options near you at HappyCow.net.

For our environment

Animals raised for agriculture and sealife caught from the ocean contribute to the environmental crisis. See for yourself on Netflix:

  • Cowspiracy
  • Seaspiracy

For the animals

They suffer immensely in all industries from food, clothing, cosmetics, science, and entertainment. If you were in their place, then what would you want? See for yourself on YouTube:

  • Earthlings
  • Dominion
Visit your local animal sanctuary so you can see they're like us in the ways that matter.
